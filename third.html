About us

Company was founded in 2009 by Mehar Adeel and now is a renowned name in the Marbles and Granites industry.

Main activity is providing marbles, granites ,mosaics and related products in various colors, qualities and from different origins around the world to cater the needs of our valuable consumers, dealers and projects. Integrity, customer service, quality and business with a conscience are the main theme of the Company. It has been able to solicit and cater to dealers and consumers from all parts of Pakistan.

Over a period of more than four decades, the company has carved out a name for itself as a trusted and reliable supplier in the ever-demanding stone market.
