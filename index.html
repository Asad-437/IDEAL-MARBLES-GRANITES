Why choose Ideal Marbles and Granites ?

When it comes to choosing the right material for your flooring, marble and granite are both popular choices. While both are beautiful, durable, and versatile, they do have some key differences that you should consider before making your decision.
Granite is a natural stone that is quarried from the earth's crust. It is known for its hardness and durability, making it an ideal choice for high-traffic areas like kitchens and entryways. Granite comes in a variety of colors and patterns, and its unique variations make each piece one-of-a-kind. Plus, it's resistant to heat, scratches, and stains, making it a low-maintenance choice for busy households.
Marble, on the other hand, is also a natural stone, but it has a more delicate and elegant appearance. It is often used in areas like bathrooms and bedrooms where its luxurious look can be appreciated. However, marble is more prone to scratches and stains than granite, so it requires a bit more upkeep.
When it comes to flooring, both marble and granite are excellent choices. They both add value to your home and are sure to impress guests. However, if you're looking for a low-maintenance option that can withstand heavy use, granite may be the better choice. If you want a luxurious look and don't mind a bit of upkeep, marble may be the way to go.
